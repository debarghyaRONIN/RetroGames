SUPER MARIO

Mario, a beloved video game character created by Shigeru Miyamoto, made his debut in 1981's "Donkey Kong." Originally known as "Jumpman," Mario's popularity surged in 1985 with the release of "Super Mario Bros." on the Nintendo Entertainment System (NES).

The Mario franchise diversified to sports, racing, and RPG genres with games like "Mario Kart" and "Super Mario RPG." Mario's legacy remains unparalleled, influencing gaming culture and becoming Nintendo's mascot, symbolizing joy and timeless fun.
